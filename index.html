About Priyanka Chopra

Born in Jamshedpur, India in 1982, Priyanka Chopra is a globally celebrated actress, singer, producer, and humanitarian. After winning the Miss World pageant in 2000, she rose to stardom in Bollywood, starring in hits like Barfi!, Don, and Mary Kom.

Global Impact

She broke into Hollywood with the TV series Quantico and starred in major films like Baywatch and The White Tiger. As a producer with her company Purple Pebble Pictures, she champions diverse storytelling.

Why I Admire Her

Priyanka Chopra is fearless. She’s constantly evolving, whether she's acting, producing, advocating for children’s rights with UNICEF, or starting meaningful conversations about representation. Her grace, ambition, and compassion inspire me deeply.

Major Achievements

  • Miss World 2000 winner
  • National Film Award & multiple Filmfare Awards
  • UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador for child rights
  • Included in TIME's 100 Most Influential People
